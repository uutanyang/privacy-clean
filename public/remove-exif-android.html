// FAQ
Do Android phones store EXIF data?
Yes. Android phones store GPS coordinates, camera model, lens info, aperture, ISO, focal length, flash status, date/time, and Android version in EXIF metadata.
Can someone track me through Android photos?
Yes, if GPS/Location is enabled for your camera app, your exact location is embedded in every photo. Anyone with the original file can find where it was taken.
Does Android remove EXIF when sharing?
Some apps like Google Photos strip location when sharing. But camera model, timestamps, and other metadata remain. PrivacyClean removes ALL metadata.
